Tutorial

1. Move with WASD keys (W: forward, S: backward, A: left, D: right).

2. Aim with your mouse and click to shoot alien drones.

3. Hit drones to score points; they spawn periodically and chase you.

4. Avoid walking through walls.

5. Click "Reset Game" to start over.

6. Goal: Score as many points as possible by destroying drones!
